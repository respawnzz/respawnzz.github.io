Privacy Policy

No Logs Policy

SecureNET operates a strict no-logs policy. We do not track, collect, or store any logs of your online activity while using our VPN service. Your privacy is our priority.

We only collect minimal information necessary for account management: email address and payment information. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies only for authentication and security purposes. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that compromise your privacy.
